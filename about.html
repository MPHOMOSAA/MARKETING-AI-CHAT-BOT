Our Story
Built on a Promise
of No Shortcuts.
"
I founded Diamond Aqua Pools on a single principle — no shortcuts, ever. Every pool we build is built as if my own family will swim in it for the next 20 years.

Growing up in Randburg, Marcus Van Zyl was shaped by a story that would go on to define his entire career. When Marcus was 16, a close family friend lost over R200,000 to an unlicensed contractor who demolished their existing pool — and simply disappeared. The pool was never replaced. The family was devastated financially and emotionally.

That story stayed with Marcus. He went on to study BSc Civil Engineering at the University of Pretoria with a singular purpose: to become the pool builder that family never got to meet. Someone with real qualifications, real accountability, and an absolute zero-tolerance policy for cutting corners.

In 2012, Marcus founded Diamond Aqua Pools from a small warehouse in Randburg with one truck, two qualified technicians, and a client list of three. Today, Diamond Aqua Pools has completed over 450 premium pools across Johannesburg and Gauteng. Every single one is still standing. Every single one has a 98% satisfaction rating from the original client.

What sets Marcus apart from most contractors? He gives his direct cell number to every single client — before the project starts, during, and after handover. In an industry full of unreachable contractors and broken promises, that simple act of accountability has built a reputation that no marketing budget could buy.

To deliver the finest pool installations in South Africa — with uncompromising standards, full transparency, and the kind of personal accountability that the industry desperately needs. Every pool we build carries the Diamond Aqua name, and we never forget that.
